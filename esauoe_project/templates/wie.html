Women in Engineering

Empowering the next generation of female engineering leaders at University of Eldoret

Breaking Barriers, Building Futures

The Women in Engineering (WIE) initiative at ESA is dedicated to supporting, empowering, and celebrating female engineering students. We provide a platform for networking, mentorship, and professional development.

Our mission is to increase the representation of women in engineering fields and create an inclusive environment where every female engineer can thrive and reach her full potential.

Membership Requirements

  • Must be a registered student at University of Eldoret
  • Pursuing any engineering discipline
  • Commitment to attend at least 60% of WIE events
  • Willingness to participate in mentorship programs
  • Interest in promoting women in STEM fields
  • Good academic standing (GPA of 2.5 and above)
