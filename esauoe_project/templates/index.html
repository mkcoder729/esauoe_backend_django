About ESA

Discover the story behind Kenya's most dynamic engineering student community

Empowering Tomorrow's Engineering Leaders

The Engineering Students' Association (ESA) at the University of Eldoret stands as a beacon of innovation, excellence, and collaboration in Kenya's engineering education landscape. Founded in 2013, we've grown to become one of the most influential student organizations in East Africa.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world engineering challenges. Through workshops, industry partnerships, and hands-on projects, we prepare our members for successful careers in various engineering disciplines.

Our Mission

Committed to establishing a space for academic prowess, professional growth and communal involvement among engineering students.
